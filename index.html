ABOUT COMPANY

Paramount Propack Pvt. Ltd.

Paramount Propack Pvt. Ltd. is one of India’s leading manufacturers of industrial packaging solutions. Through Autorack, we deliver specialized plastic crates and customized partitions built exclusively for the automotive sector.

Our products are designed to:

  • Optimize storage and in-plant movement
  • Protect valuable components
  • Reduce handling damage and losses
